how we work

Our Process at Studio Designbox Pvt. Ltd. our work

We use a specific step-by-step approach in providing the clients with creative, functional and top quality interior solutions meeting every clients need.

Discovery & Consultation

We start by getting to know your vision, style, space, and budget to lay a solid foundation for your interior project.

Concept & Design Development

Our design team creates mood boards, layouts, and visual 3D images to bring the interior ideas to life in a visual and practical manner.

Execution & Build
